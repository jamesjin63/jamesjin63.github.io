The World Within Data

Jin-xin Zheng is a Ph.D. candidate at The National Institute of Parasitic Diseases China CDC. He got his bachelor’s degree from Anhui Medical University in 2015, enrolled one year of master’s training program in Nanjing Medical University and joined Jiangsu Institute of Parasitic Institute in 2016 to finish the master program. He has three years of study experience in Public Health, especially in bio-statistics, modeling methods within Infectious diseases. And cooperated with the clinician for trail setup and data analysis, by applying machine learning methods to clinical researches. With passion in R and Python statistics tools for medical data analysis and visualizations.
